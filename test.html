Introduction

What distinguishes a software developer from a development team lead? As a team leader, you are now in charge of people, their performance, and motivation. Your output is the output of your team.

In this course, you'll learn what does it mean to be an exceptional team leader and how to become one. ↵You won't find boring theories, "let's be Agile!" mantras or easy-to-Google ideas here. In practical, down-to-earth style, you'll learn how to:

  • Increase team's throughput

Frequently asked questions

For whom is this course?

The course is for experienced and motivated engineers who already know how to write good code and get things done. It's for those who want to be an exceptional leader. It's for those who want to make a big impact on people and organization.

What are admission requirements for the course?

The course requires at least three years of commercial software development experience. The content of the course requires a certain level of maturity. To create effective learning environment, we invite motivated individuals whose participation is beneficial to other attendees.

I have deep technical experience, but I'm not a dev. Can I participate?

Let's figure it out together. Ask at [email protected]

How is the learning process organized?

You will work with other developers in a small team. You'll be solving real-world challenges, such as optimizing slow development process, making unhappy customers happy, finding cost-effective solutions to problems, refactoring interviewing process. We'll also address and resolve your current career, team and company challenges.

Would I receive a certificate upon completing the course?

Yes, you will receive a Certificate of Accomplishment.

What is the language of instruction?

The course is taught in English. Non-native speakers are welcome.

How would I develop my knowledge after the course?

You will receive a list of books, blogs, videos for self-development. After intensive 2-day masterclass, Eduards will be available for free, one-on-one mentoring.

How do I convince my manager to send me to this course?

Tell the truth: Coursera is for beginners, conferences are just fun and you want to learn serious stuff. You will get new skills, a certificate and tons of inspiration. If you dislike the course, you'll get your money back. No questions asked.
